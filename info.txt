Sin: Failure to love God and neighbor ("hardness of heart" JC)

Materialism: The assumption that happiness comes from having possessions and a high standard of living

Isms: The assumption that some groups are better and more deserving of rights than others

Over-Competitiveness: The assumption that your rights are more important than the rights of others

Violence: The assumption that physical or psychological harm to others is justifies to get your way

Quick Fix Mentality: The assumption that the quickest escape from pain or problems is the best solution.
